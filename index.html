Welcome to my space!

I am a PhD research scholar at the Curtin node of the International Center of Radio Astronomy Research (ICRAR); Curtin Institute of Radio Astronomy (CIRA). My current research interests are in observational 21-cm cosmology. I use radio interferometric observations from the state-of-the-art Murchison Widefield Array (MWA) to detect the relic 21-cm signal from the early Universe through novel techniques. Apart from the 21-cm cosmology (i.e. mainly the Cosmic-Dawn and Epoch of Reionisation (CD-EoR)), my broader interests are in fast radio bursts (FRBs) and studying Exoplanets (sseeking collaborations).
